CAREERS
Interns and University Grads
At Aldoric, we believe that great ideas can come from anyone—including you. Join us as we democratize good design for every ambitious entrepreneur, artist, or visionary with a dream. After all, Aldoric was built in a dorm room. What will you build here?
Interns

Our summer internship program is based in New York City. As an intern, you will work on a small team with full-time engineers. You’ll be paired with a Lead who, along with the Campus team, will support you through weekly one-on-one meetings and transparent feedback. In addition to working on impactful projects, you will participate in educational and social programming.

Learn More See Open Roles
University Grads

As a new grad at Squarespace, you will be empowered to help our customers succeed from day one. You’ll be embedded within a small team of engineers of all levels and will contribute directly to our suite of web products. Mentorship, detailed code reviews, and collaboration with engaged teammates will help you ramp up quickly.

Learn More See Open Roles
Engineering Internship Program

From tech talks with our SVP of Engineering to a baking class at Momofuku, you’ll finish your summer with a deep understanding of Squarespace and an appreciation for all New York has to offer. Additional perks include free lunch, a beautiful rooftop, and weekly happy hours.

“I always felt like I was truly part of the team at Aldoric. People would listen to my thoughts and feedback, even if it wasn't related to my project. Everyone wanted to help me succeed.”
