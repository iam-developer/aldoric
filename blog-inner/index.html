What're Inside My Badge
May 22, 2020 · 15 min read · By Lara C.
You care about writing clean code. You think of web development in terms of product engineering. You’re experienced in building and delivering custom web applications, websites and things we haven’t thought of yet. You’re comfortable with PHP, Javascript and modern web technologies.

You communicate well where it counts (and you’re not afraid of a little documentation); you thrive on collaboration, and manage your time and priorities with ease. Most importantly, you’re committed to delivering high-quality outcomes for your colleagues, clients and end users.

Put it this way: You’re a motivated web developer with 3+ years’ experience; you love learning new things, and want to work with people who care about quality.

Aldoric is a close-knit team of creative professionals, focused on delivering best-in-class results for clients all over the world. And when it comes to hiring, we commit to culture first. We turn up each day because we care about what we do, and believe an enjoyable, creative workplace with like-minded people is the secret to high-quality strategy and output.

As a Web Designer, your expertise and perspective will help ensure all the products we ship meet high standards of best practice, innovation and user experience. We’re passionate about all things digital here, and encourage you to indulge your passions, get creative, and feel free to experiment and explore.

You care about writing clean code. You think of web development in terms of product engineering. You’re experienced in building and delivering custom web applications, websites and things we haven’t thought of yet. You’re comfortable with PHP, Javascript and modern web technologies.

What do you need to do?
  • Create robust digital platforms using contemporary technologies and methodologies, prioritising efficiency, readability, reusability and systems thinking.

  • Apply any previous knowledge and experience of PHP, MySQL, JavaScript, Git, Laravel (or other frameworks), CMS and API integration, WordPress, Magento, React/Vue/Angular, and DevOps to a variety of projects to serve a range of industries.

  • Contribute to the ongoing evolution of our homegrown products and internal development processes.

  • Collaborate with designers, developers, writers, managers, clients and users to create world-class digital experiences.

  • Work closely with our development team on strategy and product development. An ability to give and take constructive feedback is essential.
